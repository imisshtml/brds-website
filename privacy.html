Your privacy is important to us. It is imisshtml LLC's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating the BRDS Care platform and its associated website (brds.care). We have outlined our privacy practices below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated as you use our platform, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, company/practice name, role, and login credentials when you register for or use BRDS Care.
  • Contact & Inquiry Data: Information submitted through our consultation or contact forms, including name, company, phone, country, industry, and any questions or notes you provide.
  • Usage Data: Log data, IP addresses, browser type, pages viewed, and actions taken within the platform, collected automatically to improve service reliability and performance.
  • Clinical & Practice Data: Data entered by authorized healthcare providers and staff into the BRDS Care platform. This may include Protected Health Information (PHI) subject to HIPAA regulations (see Section 5).
  • Billing Information: Payment and insurance billing data processed through our integrated billing services. Full payment card data is handled by PCI-compliant third-party processors and is never stored directly by imisshtml LLC.

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect personal information by lawful and fair means and use it solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and improve the BRDS Care platform and its features.
  • To respond to consultation requests, support inquiries, and communications.
  • To process billing and insurance claims on behalf of authorized practices.
  • To send service-related notifications, updates, and, where you have opted in, marketing communications.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations, including HIPAA and other healthcare regulations.
  • To detect, prevent, and address security incidents and fraudulent activity.

Before or at the time of collecting personal information, we identify the purposes for which information is being collected. We will not use your data for purposes beyond those stated without obtaining your consent or as required by law.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

imisshtml LLC does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors and partners (e.g., cloud infrastructure, EDI clearinghouses such as Stedi, analytics tools) who assist us in operating the platform, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Business Associates: With Business Associates under a signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as required by HIPAA when PHI is involved.
  • Legal Requirements: When required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, where user data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the same privacy commitments.

4. Data Accuracy & Retention

Personal data should be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used and, to the extent necessary for those purposes, should be accurate, complete, and up-to-date. We provide mechanisms within the platform for authorized users to review and update their information.

We will only retain personal information for as long as necessary for the fulfilment of the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Upon account termination or written request, we will delete or anonymize your personal data in accordance with our retention schedule and legal obligations.

5. HIPAA Compliance & Protected Health Information

BRDS Care operates as a platform used by healthcare providers who may store and process Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA) and its implementing regulations.

  • imisshtml LLC maintains a dedicated, on-staff HIPAA-Compliance Security Officer responsible for overseeing our HIPAA compliance program.

If you are a covered entity and require a BAA, please contact us at info@imisshtml.com.
